About Our Project

Heart disease is a leading cause of death worldwide, and early detection is crucial for prevention. CardiacScan AI leverages machine learning and deep learning to analyze patient health data and provide early risk assessments, aiding medical professionals and individuals in making informed health decisions.

Our AI-powered platform processes multiple data points to identify patterns that may indicate potential cardiac issues before they become life-threatening, significantly improving preventive care possibilities.

Our Approach

Data Collection

Gathering relevant patient health data from diverse sources to ensure comprehensive analysis.

Data Processing

Cleaning and normalizing datasets for accuracy and consistency in predictions.

Model Development

Implementing advanced ML and DL algorithms optimized for cardiac health analysis.

Evaluation

Using precision, recall, and F1-score metrics for rigorous performance assessment.

Future Directions

We aim to integrate advanced deep learning models such as CNNs and LSTMs to enhance our prediction capabilities. Our roadmap includes:

  • Implementing real-time prediction capabilities for continuous monitoring
  • Expanding our datasets to improve accuracy across diverse populations
  • Developing mobile applications for accessible heart health monitoring
  • Creating API integrations with existing healthcare systems
